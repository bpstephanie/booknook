About BookNook

Welcome to BookNook! We are so happy to have you here!

How did we start? Well, first of all, we are a book-loving bunch who realised during the lockdown that we lacked a community to share our passion with.

Our Mission

At BookNook, our mission is to create a welcoming community for all book lovers, where everyone can discover, share, and celebrate the joy of reading.

Our Values

  • Inclusivity: We welcome everyone, from avid readers to newbies.
  • Community: We believe in the power of sharing and connecting through books.
  • Passion: Our love for books drives everything we do.

Giving Back

We're proud to partner with local charities to promote literacy and make books accessible to everyone. Every purchase you make helps support these initiatives, so you can feel good about your love for books making a positive impact.

Press & Awards

We're proud to have been featured in various media outlets and to have received several awards for our contributions to the reading community.

So, whether you're a seasoned bookworm or just starting your reading journey, come join us at BookNook. Let's explore, discover, and celebrate the wonderful world of books together!
