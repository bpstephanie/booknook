Our Partners

At BookNook, we believe in the power of reading and education to transform lives. We are proud to partner with various charities and local councils to promote literacy, support education, and donate books to those in need. Learn more about our partners and the incredible work they do:

National Literacy Trust

The National Literacy Trust is an independent charity that works with schools and communities to give children the literacy skills they need to succeed in life. They empower people with the literacy skills necessary to change their stories and improve their lives.

Visit National Literacy Trust

Learn2Love2Read

Learn2Love2Read is a charity dedicated to helping children develop a love for reading. They provide early years classes, training, and events to support parents and children in their reading journey. Their programs focus on interactive reading sessions, where volunteers read with children, helping them improve their reading skills and fostering a lifelong love for books. Learn2Love2Read believes that reading is essential for both the heart and soul, and they strive to make reading accessible and enjoyable for all children.

Visit Learn2Love2Read

Book Trade Entry Support

Book Trade Entry Support is a charity dedicated to helping young people start their careers in the book trade. They offer financial assistance for interview costs, paid internships, and work experience opportunities. Their support includes covering expenses such as travel, subsistence, rent, and clothing, making it easier for individuals to pursue their passion for books and publishing. Additionally, they provide subsidized housing for new entrants to the book trade, ensuring that financial barriers do not hinder their career aspirations

Visit Book Trade Entry Support
